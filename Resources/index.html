Introduction to the Dashboard

The video below provides an overview of the Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program. Watch this video to learn how to use this website for state-level monitoring of alcohol selling establishments. The map featured in this example can be found on the State-wide Data tab, located in the far left of the menu at the top of the screen.


Harm Reduction

This video provides several examples of how the Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program can be used to identify strategies to reduce alcohol related harms in Iowa communities. The map featured in this example can be found on the State-wide Data tab, located in the far left of the menu at the top of the screen.


Cedar Rapids, Iowa Alcohol Establishments

The Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program also includes a tab where the communities of Cedar Rapids, Marshalltown, Burlington, and Iowa City can view their alcohol establishments in context of community calls for service, or 911 calls. This video introduces findings from the community of Cedar Rapids, Iowa and it explores the relationship between violence-related calls for service and alcohol establishments. The map featured in this example can be found on the Community Data tab, located along the left of the menu at the top of the screen.


Iowa City, Iowa Alcohol Establishments

This video introduces the Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program's findings from the community of Iowa City, Iowa. This video reviews findings related to family-related calls for service and alcohol establishments. The map featured in this example can be found on the Community Data tab, located along the left of the menu at the top of the screen.


Marshalltown, Iowa Alcohol Establishments

This video introduces the Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program's findings from the community of Marshalltown, Iowa. This video highlights findings about alcohol-related calls for service, alcohol establishments, and the alcohol vulnerability index. The map featured in this example can be found on the Community Data tab, located along the left of the menu at the top of the screen.


Burlington, Iowa Alcohol Establishments

This video introduces the Iowa Alcohol Density Monitoring Program's findings from the community of Burlington, Iowa. This video reviews findings related to alcohol establishments and alcohol-related calls for service, as well as discusses potential community policies that can help reduce these calls. The map featured in this example can be found on the Community Data tab, located along the left of the menu at the top of the screen.
